The Eco-QR project was created to address key environmental challenges related to plastic waste in Kazakhstan. Our goal is gamified motivation for conscious plastic disposal, providing easy access to collection points, and increasing the level of eco-awareness.

Key Project Values:

  • Convenience and Simplicity: Dropping off plastic should be as simple as scanning a QR code.
  • Transparency and Rewards: Every action is rewarded with points that can be exchanged for prizes in the Extra Zone.
  • Eco-Education: We provide information on the importance of recycling and proper sorting.
  • Contribution to Society: Improving ESG reporting and reducing the carbon footprint.

How Eco-QR Works: A Simple Path to Recycling

Find a Point

Use the map to find the nearest plastic bottle collection point in your city. Choose the closest ones!

Scan QR

At the collection point, launch the scanner on the website and recognize the unique QR code to start the drop-off procedure.

Photo Verification

Take a photo of yourself dropping off the bottle. This is needed to verify your action and accrue bonuses.

Bonuses and Extra Zone

Get points! Exchange them for prizes, discounts, and goods in the special zone from Coca-Cola.
